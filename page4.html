ENHANCING CYBERSECURITY AWARENESS
Cybersecurity awareness involves staying alert to online risks. It includes recognizing unusual account activity, suspicious emails, or unexpected pop-ups, and using tools like antivirus software, secure Wi-Fi, and privacy settings. Being informed about evolving cyber threats enables smarter online decisions and faster responses, strengthening overall digital security and preventing attacks before they happen.
