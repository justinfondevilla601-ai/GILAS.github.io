UNDERSTANDING ONLINE THREATS
The internet offers many opportunities but also exposes users to risks like phishing, malware, scams, hacking, and identity theft. Phishing tricks people into sharing private information, while malware can steal data or damage devices. Online scams often pretend to be legitimate offers or contacts. By recognizing warning signs such as suspicious links, unknown senders, and untrustworthy websites, users can better protect themselves from online threats.
