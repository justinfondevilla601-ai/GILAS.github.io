BUILDING SAFE DIGITAL HABITS
Safe digital habits help users stay secure online. These include using strong, unique passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, avoiding unsafe websites, and thinking before sharing personal information.Regularly updating apps and devices fixes vulnerabilities, and cautious clicking reduces the risk of hacking or scams.Practicing these habits protects personal information and contributes to a safer digital environment.
